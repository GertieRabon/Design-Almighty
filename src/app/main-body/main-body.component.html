About Us

The Design Almighty is a creative brand that expresses art through designs that show profound personality and appreciation for the arts. Established in 2019, the brand began its journey to create designs that will help people express their different moods and interests. The brand also aims to promote the local and artistic works from the Philippines. The Design Almighty offers a variety of novelty items such as pins, mirrors, stickers, and prints.
